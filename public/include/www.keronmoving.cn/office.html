企业搬迁Office Move

KERON国际了解办公室及厂房的搬迁难度更高、更加繁琐，困难在于需要参与的各个部门有效的、良好的、积极的协调在一起，并保证正常经营活动的顺利进行。我们的专业操作团队，深刻理解客户的这一需求，以快捷、效率、整洁为服务指引，使客户能在迁入新址后以最快的速度开展工作。

The logistics involved in an office move is usually a lot more complicated than when moving a home. In addition to the size of the move, the importance of having each department perfectly reorganized to its desired state, the value of the documents, and the respect of deadlines are crucial in order not to disrupt the company’s activity. At KERON, we understand how important an office move is for a company. This is why everything will be arranged so that 9am on Monday, every employee can start the day as if it were any other Monday.

1、免费测量评估Free Pre-Move Survey

您可以通过网站或者免费服务热线与我们的客服专员取得联系，我们将对您公司内需要搬运的物品进行仔细清点和评估，并了解出发地和搬入地的具体情况，以便制定最快捷的运输路线。

During the survey, your office move consultant will go through your office to estimate the volume of the move. Also he or she will become to understand the needs both at origin and destination specific to your company and its office.

2、完善准备工作Pre-Move Preparation

在准备搬迁工作期间，KERON国际的客服专员会对各类准备工作进行指引，并指导公司员工使用我们提供的标签贴进行分类打包。

During the pre-move preparation, your staff will be briefed on the moving steps, each one responsibility, self-packing instructions, explanation of the codes and labels to be out on the moving boxes.

3、开始搬迁Moving Day

进行最后的打包整理并装车运输，从旧址迁入新址后，我们的团队还会为您提供拆包、分发物品和陈列摆放工作。旧址内遗留的杂物和垃圾我们会清扫干净。

The moving itself will start with the packing and possible disassembling of furniture by KERON’s movers. Will then follow the transportation, the delivery, the distribution and the unpacking with reassembling at destination. At the end of the move, packing materials, debris and protections will be removed by our care in order to make place for your personnel.

文件及档案Documentation & Records Management

文件及各类档案请先使用我们提供的标签贴预先分类及整理，以便迁入新办公室后方便拆包。

Documents and all types of files should be pre sorted and arranged by our labels so that they can be unpacked immediately after moving into the new office.

贵重物品Valuables

员工的私人物品请妥善整理并随身携带，公司的贵重物品整理妥当后，请放入保险箱内。

Please tidy up your personal belongings and bring them with you. Please put them in the safe when the valuables are arranged

需要搬迁物品Removal items required

需要搬走的物品请进行标记，以便我们的操作团队迅速装车，不要的文件档案等请妥善处理，家具等大件可请我们的团队代为处理。

Please mark the items that need to be removed so that our operation team will be able to load the car quickly. No files or files should be properly handled. Furniture and other large items can be dealt with by our team instead.
